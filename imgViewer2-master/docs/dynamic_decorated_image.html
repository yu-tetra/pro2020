imgViewer2 Plugin - dynamic decorated image example

This example demonstrates the widget with a decorated (borders and padding) dynamic width image. Zoom in and out using the mousewheel, +/- controls, double click/tap or hold the shift key and drag out a rectangle. Left mouseclick and drag to pan. On touch enable devices pinch gestures can be used to zoom in and out and tap and drag to pan around.

Resize the window and try again.
